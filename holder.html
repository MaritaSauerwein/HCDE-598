Clamp design

This page describes how to make the clamp that supports the fixture of laser cut parts in an Ultimaker.

Bill of materials
component quantity
3.3 mm plywood around 250mm x150 mm
PLA filament 1 spoil
M2X5mm bolts for triangle: 62, every aditional plate +18
M2X0.4mm heat-set inserts for plastic for triangle: 44, every aditional plate +12
M10 Threaded Rod, 225mm 1
M10 wing nuts 2

All components are either made with lasercutting or 3D printing and the round component in the middle is a combination of both.

Number component quantity
1 little clamp 4 for each plate (12 for a triangular shape)
2 middle part 2
3 positioning stick 2 for each plate (6 for a triangular shape)
4 printbed clamp 2
5 axis clamp 2
6 vertical stick 4 (glue two together)
